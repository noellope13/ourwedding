Our Love Story

First Met

January 2017

Rachel and Noel first met while working together at Salsas. They were friends for six months, getting to know each other and making fun memories along the way. They bonded over video games, which is when their competativeness began. Both of them also love talking about video editing, movies and had the same taste in music. They discovered they have alot in common and started hanging more often. As they continued getting to know each other better, they were inseparable and grew closer together.

First Date

June 2017

Noel thought of taking Rachel to the Strand during an annual art walk for their first official date. While they were there, Rachel took Noel to her favorite restaurant in that area, the All Star Drug Store. The two of them got cheeseburgers and split a milkshake, then they took a stroll to LaKings and the Chocolate Factory, where they got some sweet treats. A couple of weeks later, Noel had an idea after remembering Rachel's favorite animal. He took her on a dolphin tour boat ride, where he asked her to be his girlfriend. Of course Rachel had to say 'YYYAAASSS'!

Promise Day

June 2018

After a year of being together, Rachel and Noel decided to celebrate by taking a road trip to San Antonio. It was always on Rachel's bucket list to swim with dolphins, but that wasn't the only highlight of their anniversary. Noel had been preparing to surprise her with an unforgattable token of his love for her. Both of them had a unique lunch at 750-foot-tall restaurant, the Tower of the Americas, where Noel gifted Rachel with a promise ring and poem. Noel had planned and waited for this day for almost a full year.

Engagement Day

December 2022

Noel tricked Rachel into believing she was secretly helping the family plan a surprise birthday lunch for him. She was so excited to be apart of his surprise, little did she know he was the one who was planning with family to surprise her with the proposal. Noel coordinated with both families and close friends to be on the seawall at the dolphin statue, with a set of LED letters that read..Marry Me!!... She had no idea what was about to happen, on their way to where she thought his was surprise birthday lunch was supposed to be. Noel had a friend make a fake phone call for help with his car. Rachel was so worried because this was going to put them behind schedule of when they were to meet with everyone. Right before heading to meet his friend, Noel pulled over to the seawall and handed her a small gift he had hidden in his truck. Rachel was very confused after opening it because inside the box was a puzzle piece. He asked her to put it on a blindfold because he had a little surprise for her. Once she put it on, Noel lead Rachel to awaiting family and friends. When Noel told Rachel to remove the blindfold, she saw everyone there and they shouted SURPRISE!! When she turned around, Noel was on one knee with the ring in hand. He then popped the question and she was confused seeing all the people there, because it took a minute for her to realize what was happening. Soon after there came the tears of happiness, hugs all around, and of course a YES to his question.

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

Dress Code: Semi-Formal
Semi-formal is more dressy than casual beach wear, but not as fancy as black tie since the venue is non-traditional. Certain attire can make you feel restricted and overheated in a warm/tropical area. Our suggestions for the gentlemen would be to wear slacks and a button-down shirt. For the ladies, we recommend either wearing slacks and light blouse or a tea length or summer dress, both are great options, plus they will transition beautifully into our reception venue. We ask all guests to avoid wearing jeans or shorts. Please also keep in mind the bride should be the only one wearing white.

WHERE SHOULD I PARK?

The venue has its own private parking lot available to our guests. There will be plenty of spots on site. Please note the parking lot is a dirt and gravel lot.

WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?

If you're attending to the ceremony, please arrive at least 30 minutes early to ensure everyone has arrived by the time the ceremony starts, which will be indicated on your formal invitation and our website.

DO YOU HAVE A REGISTRY?

No, but we hope you consider contributing to some of our first experiences and memories that we will be making together as husband and wife. We have created a Honeymoon Fund option. If you choose to gift us a contribution, you can either use our website to do so or bring your gift contribution with you on our big day. We will have a Honeymoon Chest available to all of our guests. After many years together, we have no need for physical gifts. Thank you for your understanding. You can find more information about our Honeymoon Fund on our website.
