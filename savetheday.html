WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

Dress Code: Semi-Formal
Semi-formal is more dressy than casual beach wear, but not as fancy as black tie since the venue is non-traditional. Certain attire can make you feel restricted and overheated in warm/tropical weather.
Gentlemen should wear slacks and a button-down shirt. Ladies should wear short or tea length dresses, which are great because they won't drag in the sand, plus they will transition beautifully into our reception venue. We ask all guests to avoid wearing jeans, shorts, and the color white or any other colors resembling it.
