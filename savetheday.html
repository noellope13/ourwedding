WHERE IS SURFSIDE?

Location:
Surfside, Texas is situated on the Texas Gulf Coast in Brazoria County. It is approximately 65 miles south of Houston and 30 miles east of Galvestion.

How to Get There:
From Houston, you can take State Highway 288 south toward Lake Jackson and then follow Texas State Highway 332 until you reach Surfside Beach. The drive takes approximately 1.5 hours, depending on traffic and your starting location within Houston.
From most locations on Galveston Island, head northeast toward the San Luis Pass Bridge on Seawall Blvd (also known as Termini-San Luis Pass Rd).NOTE: There will be a $2.00 toll fee when crossing the bridge. Continue on Seawall Blvd, which merges into Bluewater Highway (Texas State Highway 3005). Drive along Bluewater Highway for approximately 30-35 minutes.
