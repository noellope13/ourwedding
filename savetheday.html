WHERE IS SURFSIDE?

Location:
Surfside, Texas is situated on the Texas Gulf Coast in Brazoria County. It is approximately 65 miles south of Houston and 30 miles east of Galvestion.

How to Get There:
From Houston, you can take State Highway 288 south toward Lake Jackson and then follow Texas State Highway 332 until you reach Surfside Beach. The drive takes approximately 1.5 hours, depending on traffic and your starting location within Houston.

From most locations on Galveston, head northeast toward the San Luis Pass Bridge on Seawall Blvd (also known as Termini-San Luis Pass Rd).NOTE: There will be a $2.00 toll fee when crossing the bridge. Continue on Seawall Blvd, which merges into Bluewater Highway (Texas State Highway 3005). Drive along Bluewater Highway for approximately 30-35 minutes.

WHAT IS THE DRESS CODE?

Dress Code: Semi-Formal
Semi-formal is more dressy than casual beach wear, but not as fancy as black tie since the venue is non-traditional. Certain attire can make you feel restricted and overheated in warm/tropical weather.
Gentlemen should wear slacks and a button-down shirt. Ladies should wear short or tea length dresses, which are great because they won't drag in the sand, plus they will transition beautifully into our reception venue. We ask all guests to avoid wearing jeans, shorts, and the color white or any other colors resembling it.
